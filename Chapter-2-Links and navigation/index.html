Shin Godzilla

Shin Godzilla is a 2016 Japanese kaiju film directed by Hideaki Anno and Shinji Higuchi. It is the 31st film in the Godzilla franchise and the first reboot of the series, portraying Godzilla's first appearance in contemporary Japan.

Key Information

Director: Hideaki Anno, Shinji Higuchi

Release Date: July 29, 2016

Running Time: 120 minutes

Cast and Creative Team

  • Hiroki Hasegawa as Rando Yaguchi
  • Yutaka Takenouchi as Hideki Akasaka
  • Satomi Ishihara as Kayoko Ann Patterson
  • Music by Shiro Sagisu

Characters

  • Godzilla - The giant monster terrorizing Japan
  • Rando Yaguchi - Deputy Chief Cabinet Secretary
  • Hideki Akasaka - Special Advisor to the Prime Minister
  • Kayoko Ann Patterson - US special envoy

Reviews
